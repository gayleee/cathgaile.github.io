Popular Destinations

Berlin
The capital city of Germany

Explore the marvels of Berlin! In the broad glacial valley of the Spree River, the city is located in the center of the North German Plain. Its neoclassical buildings, like the Brandenburg Gate and Berliner Church, are a must-visit. Also visit the Reichstag's glass dome and the Berlin Wall to learn more about the history of these iconic landmarks.

Hamburg
The second largest city in Germany

Come and explore Hamburg! It is surrounded by Schleswig-Holstein to the north and Lower Saxony to the south. The Port of Hamburg is Germany's largest and Europe's third-largest. Places like the Hafen City, or the connected buildings on the Glockengießerwall—the Hamburg Art Hall—offer relaxing experiences.

Dresden
The third largest city in eastern Germany

Say hello and discover the wonders of Desdren! It lies in the broad basin of the Elbe River between Meissen and Pirna, south of Berlin. Places like the Procession of Princes, an intricate mural depicting Saxon rulers, or taking a walk through the Great Garden offer relaxing experiences.
